Chicken Tetrazzini

Description

yummy :)

Ingredients

  • 3c. Diced chicken
  • 6oz. Spaghetti
  • 1/2c. Butter
  • 1/2c. Flour
  • 2c. Evaporated milk
  • 2c. Chicken broth
  • 1c. Parmesan cheese
  • 4 oz. Mushrooms (optional)
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Heat oven to 350Â°f
  2. Boil spaghetti noodles until fully cooked
  3. In a skillet, melt butter and flour over medium-low heat until bubbling, stirring frequently
  4. Whisk evaporated milk and broth into roux
  5. Add spaghetti, chicken, and mushrooms (optional)
  6. Add mixture to 9x13 baking dish, top with parmesan cheese
  7. Place in oven, uncovered for 35-55 minutes, or until top is golden-brown
